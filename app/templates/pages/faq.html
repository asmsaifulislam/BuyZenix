Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about shopping with BuyZenix.

How do I place an order? ▼
Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay via Cash on Delivery when your order arrives.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We currently support Cash on Delivery (COD). Simply pay the delivery person when your order arrives at your doorstep.
How long does delivery take? ▼
Standard Delivery: 3–5 business days (৳60)
Express Delivery: 1–2 business days (৳120)
Can I return or exchange a product? ▼
Yes! If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, contact us within 3 days of delivery. We'll arrange a return or exchange at no extra cost.
Do you offer warranty on products? ▼
Warranty coverage depends on the product and brand. Check the product description for warranty details. For electronics, most items come with a manufacturer's warranty.
How do I create an account? ▼
Click "Sign In" in the top-right corner and create an account with your email. Having an account lets you track orders, save wishlist items, and leave reviews.
How can I track my order? ▼
After placing your order, you can view the order status in your account under "My Orders". You'll also receive updates via email/SMS.
